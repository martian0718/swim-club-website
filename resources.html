Conduct Guidelines

If you are a returning athlete and have already filled out this form, you do not need to fill it out again. New athletes must fill this out to participate in practices and meets.

Informed Consent & Release of Liability
Informed Consent & Release of Liability

If you are a returning athlete and have already filled out this form, you do not need to fill it out again. New athletes must fill this out to participate in practices and meets.

ImPACT Testing
ImPACT Testing

All new participants must sign up for the ImPACT Testing which is a concussion test which takes about 30 minutes. If you are a returning athlete, and have already taken the baseline test, do not sign up for it again. You must take this test within 60 days of the start of Fall Quarter.
